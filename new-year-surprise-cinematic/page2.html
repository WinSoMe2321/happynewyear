I didn’t want to step into a new year without speaking from my heart.

Not loudly, not dramatically — just honestly.

Last year taught me many things, but the most important one was you.

Your presence has a quiet way of making everything feel steady.

In moments of doubt, you became my calm.

In moments of happiness, you became my reason.

I don’t say it enough, but I notice everything you do, everything you are.

Your patience, your care, the way you stay — it all matters to me.

Loving you hasn’t been loud, but it has been real.

You are not just someone I love… you are someone I’m grateful for.

Every prayer I make somehow carries your name.

Because you are everything I pray for.

And this is only the beginning of what I want to share with you.
